Volunteer

take responsibility for things

don't wait to have tasks assigned to you

It's OK to say you don't know the answer

Best case: "It's fixed already"

2nd best: "I know what the problem is, and it will be fixed before 12.00 today"

3rd best: "I'm working on fixing the problem, and I'll update you later"

Worst case scenario: no answer

Don’t expect “experts” to solve the problems for you

Experts are just people who have thought about a problem for longer than you [Niels Bohr: "An expert is a person who has found out by his own painful experience all the mistakes that one can make in a very narrow field."](https://en.wikiquote.org/wiki/Niels_Bohr)

especially things people aren’t very good at

repetitive tasks
